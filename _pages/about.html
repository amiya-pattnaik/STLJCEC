About Us

Saint Louis Jagannath Cultural and Educational Centeris (STLJCEC) a non-profit organization and as such all donations for STLJCEC will be towards construction of new shrine of Jagannath and cultural activities are fully tax-deductible, to the extent allowed by law.!
